New adventures with Crestron

2009-07-08

We are working on the largest Crestron project we ever head. It is going to be 8 zones of HD video switching and 34 zones of distributed audio. We are going to control it with a pair of 37" touchscreen tvs and 22 RTi remotes. We are going to be doing some tricks to hide electronics without losing control.

Look forward to more information and hopefully some freebies and tutorials.
